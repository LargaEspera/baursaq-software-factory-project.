  • Topic 2

  • As a Guest - How can I sign in

    On page HOME by clicking first button "Enter As A Guest" you can authomatically sign in, and go to Guest Recipe List Page.

  • As a Guest - What abilities Guest has?

    By entering as a Guest you do not need to sign up, then sign in. Just enter the button on HOME page. Then you can view all posts of recipes. You are able to move to all recipe pages. You can only view.

  • As a Guest - What abilities Guest does not have?

    The functionalities as having personal page, edit, delete, updatenot available.

  • Topic 3

  • New Posts - How to create new post?

    To create new post move to personal profile and click "Create New Post" button. Then you have to fill the form about your post.

  • New Posts - Can Guests add new recipes?

    Guest can`t create posts, they are only able to view recipes.

  • New Posts - What are parts of post?

    For uploading new post you have to enter recipe title, write description of food and give instructions how to cook.

  • New Posts - Is it available to delete exist posts?

    For Users deleting posts is possible. Users delete their own recipe posts on personal profile by clicking "delete" button. Guests can only read, from there they can`t delete posts.

  • Topic 4

  • Registration/Authorization - What information should be entered?

    For registration: username, password, repeat the password, name, surname.

  • Registration/Authorization - What syntax must be when entering username?

    Email must be written with @ symbol, otherwise there will be error.

  • Registration/Authorization - Why appears "Bad" Page?

    "Bad" page appears when error appears: entering wrong information while doing registration/authorization, clicking to not right buttons, when quick moving between pages.
