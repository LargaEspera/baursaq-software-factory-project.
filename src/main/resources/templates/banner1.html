Lean pancakes with apple juice

Author

admin@mail.ru

Content

I want to share with you a recipe on how to cook lean pancakes with apple juice. Such pastries will be relevant for those who are fasting or for those who adhere to a special diet. Despite the fact that pancakes are lean, even children will certainly like them. Delicate, fragrant, can be served both independently and with fillings. Take note of the recipe!

Instruction

Apple juice - 1 glass Water - 1.5 cups (can be replaced with soy milk) Flour - 2 cups Honey - 2 teaspoons (or agave syrup) Soda - 1/3 teaspoon Salt - 0.5 teaspoons Vegetable oil - 3 tbsp. spoons
