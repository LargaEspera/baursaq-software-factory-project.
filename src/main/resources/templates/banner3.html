Title

Cake Napoleon

Author

zukhra@mail.ru

Content

Friends, I share this recipe with you. What I want to note is that do not replace margarine in the dough with butter. Take a good creamy margarine. Be patient and let's get started. There is nothing complicated, you just need 2-3 hours of free time and a good mood! I tell you how to cook Napoleon Cake "Family".

Instruction

Wheat flour - 3 cups (for dough, premium) Creamy margarine - 300 grams (for dough) Chicken egg - 1 piece (for dough) Soda - 0.5 teaspoons (for dough) Vinegar 9% - 0.5 Teaspoons (for dough) Boiled water - 0.5-0.7 cups (for dough, chilled) Milk - 0.5 Liter (for cream) Sugar - 3 cups (for cream) Butter - 200 Grams (for cream, fat content 82.5%) Chicken egg - 1 piece (for cream) Wheat flour - 3 tbsp. spoons (for cream) Walnuts - 50 grams (for decoration)
